Case Studies

Making a Difference Through Technology

Chef Becky – Digital Catering Made Simple

We partnered with Chef Becky, New Mexico's Chef Ambassador, to transform her catering order process. Starting from scratch, we created a fully digital version of her physical form and integrated it into a custom management dashboard. She can now update menus, manage orders, and control her offerings directly, without needing to hire a developer for routine changes. This project reflects our focus on practical solutions that put control back in the hands of our clients.

Results Delivered

  • Streamlined catering order process
  • Custom management dashboard for menu updates
  • Reduced dependency on developer for routine changes

Adovada de Mesilla – Highlighting Local Culinary Talent

Blacnova led the digital side of the first-ever Adovada de Mesilla event. We built a modern, custom website and implemented secure, encrypted live voting for attendees. We also handled registration and sponsorship tools, digital ticketing integration, and event branding to unify the experience online. The event was a success, and we are proud to have helped bring attention to Southern New Mexico's culinary traditions.

Results Delivered

  • Custom event website with secure live voting
  • Registration and sponsorship management tools
  • Digital ticketing integration
  • Unified event branding
